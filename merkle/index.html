Merkle Prize

OmiseGO awards OMG tokens to organizations, projects or developers redefining how people interact on the OmiseGO platform.

Apply now
Overview

We support companies, projects or developers from any background to create the best applications on the OmiseGO platform. The Merkle Prize aims to incentivize the creation of practical applications and solutions that demonstrate real-world use cases for blockchain technology.

Purpose
Fostering
To foster innovation and talent in the blockchain and fintech space.
Showcasing
To provide a platform for individuals, projects and organizations to showcase their applications and solutions to the world.
Accessing
To lower the barriers to entry and widen access to useful applications on the OmiseGO platform.
What OmiseGO offers

Prize Pool

1 OMG

A total of 200,000 OMG tokens will be awarded over a period of time. OMG tokens are awarded according to performance and deliverables that are to be met and provided at each stage of the competition. As tokens are unlocked at each stage, projects are expected to proceed to the next phase of their development plan. The criteria (expectations and outputs) for each stage will be defined and agreed by both parties upon being selected into the competition. For more details, please see the guidelines.

Who can apply?

Organizations, projects or developers

Deadline

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis.

Organizations, projects or individuals that reach the final stage of the Merkle Prize are expected to co-market their applications and solutions with OmiseGO. A partnership marketing agreement will be co-developed and OmiseGO’s branding guidelines and other relevant information will be provided.

While we have a large presence in Southeast Asia, our audience is global and our professional network across the blockchain and fintech space is wide. OmiseGO offers the following marketing opportunities to Merkle Prize awardees as they reach the final stage of the competition:

  • Cross-promotion through our official channels 
  • A feature article in the OmiseGO newsletter and blog 
  • Opportunity to be in an ‘Ask Me Anything’ (AMA) video with OmiseGO 
  • A podcast that dives deep into your application/solution and your company or project
  • Opportunity to attend a global event with OmiseGO Devcon 5 to showcase your application on the OmiseGO platform
